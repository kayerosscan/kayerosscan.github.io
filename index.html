About Us

Care that begins with a conversation.

Kayeross Can Foundation is a not-for-profit initiative dedicated to providing compassionate palliative care support to patients and families across India. Whether it's emotional comfort, guidance through difficult medical decisions, or simply a kind voice on the other end of the line — we are here.

Palliative care is more than medicine. It is dignity, comfort, and human presence in life's most fragile moments. We believe no one should have to walk that path alone — and never because they could not afford to.

Leadership

Meet our Founder

KP

Dr. Kritie Pasrichaa

Chairman & Founder

Dr. Kritie Pasrichaa founded Kayeross Can Foundation with a singular vision — that no patient should walk the palliative care journey alone, and no family should be turned away for lack of resources.

With years of experience in patient care, Dr. Pasrichaa leads our team with compassion, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to dignity for every life we touch. Under her guidance, the Foundation has become a trusted voice for those navigating the most difficult chapters of life.
